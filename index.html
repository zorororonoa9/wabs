The problem

AI agents are the new workforce. But there's no HR, no compliance, no security team watching them.

⚠

Invisible Threats

Prompt injection, memory poisoning, and cascading agent failures happen at machine speed. Traditional SIEM tools can't see them because they look like normal behavior.

⚠

Zero Governance

Agents make decisions, access data, and communicate with other agents. Nobody tracks which agents are authorized to do what — or whether they're compliant with regulations.

△

Wasted Spend

Organizations burn 30-40% of their LLM budget on poorly optimized prompts, wrong-sized models, and tasks that don't need AI. Nobody tells them what to fix.

△

No Audit Trail

When an agent causes damage — data leak, wrong decision, compliance violation — there's no trace of why it happened. Regulators are starting to ask questions.
